What is Workwize?

Workwize is an IT lifecycle platform that helps your team procure, deploy, manage, retrieve, and dispose of employee devices — all in one place, across multiple countries.

More than a setup
Workwize aims to bring meaningful change to how your team manages IT. The teams that get the most from onboarding come open-minded and embrace the changes that this makes possible. We bring experience and structure. You bring the vision.
A few things worth thinking about early
1

Device scope & sourcing requirements

What devices do you need, and for which countries? The earlier you have a rough picture of brands, specs, and budgets, the sooner your catalogue can be built — sourcing is usually the longest part of setup.

2

Your technical setup

Do you use an MDM (Kandji, Jamf, Intune, JumpCloud), an HRIS (HiBob, BambooHR), or SSO? Knowing what's in place — and how mature it is — helps us plan integrations early.

3

Your current IT process

How do you handle device orders, onboarding, offboarding, and retrievals today? Even rough notes help — that's what we build on.

How onboarding works
Now

Contract signed

You'll receive access to your onboarding checklist and be introduced to your Onboarding Specialist.

Week 1

Kick-off call

We'll align on your setup scope, timelines, key contacts, and walk through the onboarding process together.

Weeks 2–6

Setup & training

Weekly catch-up calls covering platform configuration, integrations (HRIS, SSO, MDM), catalogue creation, and hands-on training sessions.

~Day 45

Go live & CSM handover

Your account goes live. You're introduced to your Customer Success Manager who supports you from this point on.
